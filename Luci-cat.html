Luci, the cat that is living her best life

What breed is she?

We think Luci is a Turkish Angora x Domestic Shorthair mix. Or a Turkish Van mix. Or even a Khao Manee mix. She had a small black mark on her head when she was a kitten, but now she is fully white.

Turkish Angora

A cat breed known for its white coat and almond-shaped eyes that can be blue, green, amber, or even odd-eyed. They are highly intelligent, playful, and affectionate. They are also known for their love of climbing, often seeking out the highest points in a room to observe their surroundings.

Turkish Van

This breed is distinguished by its unique color pattern, which features a white body with colored markings restricted to the head and tail. This breed is also native to Turkey and has a short or semi-long, water-resistant coat, a testament to its love for water.

Khao Manee

Often called the "White Gem," it is a rare and ancient cat breed from Thailand, notable for its pure white coat and striking eyes, which can be blue, gold, or odd-eyed. These cats are sociable, affectionate, playful and very vocal.

Domestic Shorthair

The Domestic Shorthair is a diverse category of mixed-breed cats with short coats. Known for their wide variety of colors and body types, they are among the most common and beloved cats in homes worldwide.
